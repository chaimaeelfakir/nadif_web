About NaDifTech

NADIFTECH introduces an innovative solution centered around smart trash bins equipped with unique QR codes and advanced sensors. These smart bins are designed to streamline the waste disposal process, ensuring accurate waste segregation and incentivizing users to participate actively. By scanning the QR code on the bin with our mobile app, users can unlock the bin, dispose of their waste in the designated spots, and earn points for correct sorting. These points can be redeemed with our partnered businesses, creating a rewarding cycle of responsible waste management.
